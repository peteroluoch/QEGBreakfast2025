About Queen Esthers' Generation

"Queen Esther's Generation is a generation of people who seek peace, truth, justice prosperity and total well being of God's people. A generation of women of God who provide spiritual nourishment for themselves, their families and society as a whole, with high regard for the human race. Cutting across the board as an interdenominational, multi cultural, multi racial and multi ethnic organization, QEG endeavors to remain a welcome home for all women who will be empowered and who will in turn empower others through the Gospel of Christ."
